  • Introduction

    VMB-10 is a video wall controller box which can drive up to 10 HDMI displays with single USB3.0 cable connected to your host PC or laptop (Windows only). With VMB-10, you can accomplish any of the following video wall.

    Configurations: 3x3, 2x2, 1x2, 2x1, 3x1, 1x3, 4x1, 1x4, 3x2, 2x3, 4x2, 2x4, 5x2, and 2x5. If you need to connect more HDMI displays, for example,4x4, you can connect another VMB-10 to your host PC

  • Features

    • Supports 10 channels HDMI outputs, connected to display devices with HDMI input such as LCD monitor, projectors, TV and other display unit.

    • Multiple splicing mode: 3x3, 2x2, 1x2, 2x1, 3x1, 1x3, 4x1, 1x4, 3x2, 2x3, 4x2, 2x4, 5x2, and 2x5. Support display rotation (90, 180, 270 degree) and bezel compensation function.

    • Easy to use and install: The content can be displayed from Windows ® laptop or PC by USB 3.0 port and the video wall can be set to Clone or Extended mode (max. resolution 4K).

    • Industrial fanless design
